Overview

Godrej City Nexspace brings thoughtfully designed premium office spaces to Panvel, Mumbai’s fast-growing commercial corridor. Set within the larger Godrej City masterplan, Nexspace offers modern workspaces with efficient floor plates, excellent connectivity to Navi Mumbai and the Mumbai-Pune Expressway, and the trusted backing of Godrej Properties.

Whether you are expanding your business footprint or investing in Grade-A commercial real estate, Nexspace delivers flexible office configurations, world-class infrastructure, and a vibrant neighbourhood designed for long-term growth.

Possession: December 2031  |  Configuration: Premium Office Spaces

About Godrej Properties

Established in 1990, Godrej Properties Limited is one of India’s leading real estate companies. It became the first real estate entity in the country to achieve an ISO 9001:2000 certification for its operations. Since then, it has delivered multiple projects across major cities in India and currently operates at a scale of around 95 million sq. ft. of development.
